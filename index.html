What We Do

Well, it’s been long that we have been spreading the word “Open Source” across the globe but now it’s time to put are saying on Actions. So, we are currently running up an Organisation named as “CODEUINO” after interacting with lots of people including potential contributors around the world. It is a non-profit organisation that helps to bring amazing people together to create, develop, design and make things with Open Technologies and share work for the benefit of all..
